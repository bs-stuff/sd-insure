Why Choose Independent?

A captive agent sells for one company. An independent agent writes with many — and that difference changes everything.

🤝

Multiple Companies, One Agent

A captive agent can only offer you one company's policies. An independent agent is free to write with dozens of carriers — comparing coverage and pricing to find the best fit for you, not the insurer.

🏞

Local Knowledge

Your agent lives and works in South Dakota. They understand the unique risks — from prairie hail to Missouri River flooding — and know how to protect against them.

💪

Your Advocate When It Counts

Because independent agents aren't tied to one company, their loyalty is to you. When you file a claim, they advocate on your behalf — and if a carrier isn't treating you right, they can move your business.
